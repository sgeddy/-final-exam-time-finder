NC State Final Exam Finder


How to use:

1. Check the boxes for the days of the week your class meets.
2. Select the start time of your class for each day.
3. Click the Add Class button to add all your classes.
4. Check the Remove box then click the Remove Class button to remove a class from the table.
5. Click the Tell Me My Final Exam Times button to show your final exam times.
